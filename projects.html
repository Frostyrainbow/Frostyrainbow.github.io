Projects I worked on

These are some projects that I have personally made or worked on in my career

Diesel Legacy: The Brazen Age

  • Made in: Abare Engine
  • Type of game: 2D Fighting Game
  • Role in project: Game Tester

Diesel Legacy: The Brazen Age, is a fighting game that takes the classic 2D fighter trope and gives it a unique twist. Use the 3-lane system to quickly get around the stage to either get in close or fight from a distance. Work with a friend to create awesome team combos and help them out when they are in a pickle with 2v2 fighting action. Punch, kick, shoot, grapple, and pummel you way to the top to save the Iron City.

Dyson Defense

  • Platform made on: Unity 2022.1.15f1
  • Type of game: Tower Defense
  • Role in project: Designer and Modeler

A tower defense game in which you command the Dyson Planetary Defense System (DPDS), a three ring space structure that surrounds your home planet. Place structures on the rings of the DPDS and level them up to stop the horde of ailens trying to destroy and devour your home world. Aliens are coming for you from all angles. Quickly rotate the rings of the DPDS to move and cover your blind spots.

Gravity Flux

  • Platform made on: SDL
  • Type of game: 2D Platformer
  • Role in project: Programmer

A 2D puzzle platformer in which you take the role of an unnamed individual who has been stripped of their past memories. Having had enough of the brutal testing our subject decides it is time to escape using your new found powers of gravitational manipulation. Traverse the levels by solving puzzels and eliminate enemies using your powers and bombs to finally escape the nightmarish facility.

Physics Phestival

  • Platform made on: Unity 2021.3
  • Type of game: Educational
  • Role in project: Programmer

An Educational game to help students learn and understand how capillary and kinematic physics are used in the real world. Help the formula jellies escape by raising and lowering water levels by solving capillary equations. Launch the formula jellies to finish the stack by solving kinematic equations

Shield Ninja

  • Platform made on: Unreal Engine 4.27.2
  • Type of game: 2.5D Platformer
  • Role in project: Modeler

A twin-stick 2.5D platformer focusing on precision platforming and the ability to reflect bullets with a magical shield. Use your shield as well as a bullet time ability to reflect and take out enemies by using their own attacks against them. Use your ninja training to double jump and wall cling your way to overcome the many obstacles ahead and defeat the forces of evil
